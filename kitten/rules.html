Server Rules 📜

Please read these carefully to keep the server cozy for everyone.

  • Respect Everyone

    No toxicity, hate speech, bullying, or harassment will be tolerated. Keep chat friendly and wholesome.

    Do not break blocks placed by others, and never take items from chests without permission. Use the golden shovel to claim your land!

    X-Ray, Aimbots, Fly hacks, or any unfair advantages are strictly prohibited. Mod packs like Optifine/Sodium are allowed.
